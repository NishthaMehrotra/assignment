Who We Are

Where Academic Rigour Meets Industry Reality

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute specialising in digital, cyber and technology risk roles across regulated, high-consequence environments. We exist at the intersection of academic insight and frontline practice — ensuring that the professionals we produce are immediately effective in the real world.

Through formal partnerships with leading UK universities, IDR combines evidence-based curricula with live client projects, giving students and professionals exposure to genuine risk challenges from day one. Our deployment model means graduates don't just learn — they are placed directly into organisations that need their expertise.

Who We Serve

A Community Built Around Risk

IDR brings together students, early-career professionals, and experienced practitioners who share a commitment to understanding and managing the digital risks that define modern organisations. We serve those operating in — or aspiring to work within — the most demanding, regulated environments.

Whether you're entering the field for the first time or looking to deepen expertise in AI risk, cyber resilience, or technology governance, IDR offers structured pathways aligned to where you are in your career.
